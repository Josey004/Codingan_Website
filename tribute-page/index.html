Kobe Bean Bryant

A professional NBA player, regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. One that influenced several generations of not just basketball players, but athletes of all sports.

Timeline of Kobe's NBA career and achievements

  • 1996 - Was the 13th pick of the 1996 NBA Draft at the young age 17, by the Charlotte Hornets.The Hornets would later go on to trade him to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1997 - Became a Slam Dunk Champion in the Rookie Challenge of All-Star Weekend.
  • 1999 - Phil Jackson took over of as the Lakers head coach, Phil Jackson was a very crucial part of Kobe's career and legacy.
  • 2000 - 2002 - Kobe, Shaq along with the other Laker players would go on to dominate the NBA and add to the Laker's dynasty with a three-peat (three championship titles in a row). To this day, 21 years later, no NBA team has achieve a three-peat.
  • 2006 - Against the Toronto Raptors, Kobe scored an amazing 81 points. Which set the record for the second most points scored in a game behind Wilt Chamberlain with 100 points scored in 1962.
  • 2009 - 2010 - Both fans and sports analysts were skeptical if Kobe could win a title without the asssitance of Shaq. But, this doubtfulness would later be silenced. Kobe, Pau Gasol along with the other Laker players would go to win two consecutive champtionships. Bringing Kobe's title total to five.
  • 2016 - 2017 - Due to a long list of injuries that hindered his playing abilities. Kobe would announce his retirement in 2016. Both his Laker jerseys, 8 and 24 would also retire in 2017.
  • 2018 - A little after his retirement, Kobe won an Oscar at the 90th Academy Awards for Best Animated Short Film "Dear Basketball."
  • 2020 - On January 26th, Kobe, his daughter Ginna and several others died in a helicopter crash
  • 2021 - Kobe was posthumorously voted into the Basketball Hall of Fame
